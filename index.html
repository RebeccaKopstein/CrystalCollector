Crystal Collector!

Directions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There will be four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Your objective is to match your crystal sum to the number to match.

The value of each cyrstal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change values of each crystal.
